Norwood Child Safety Councillors News

Recent News

Stay up to date with the latest news from the Norwood Child Safety Councillors.

Donation of $10,000 received from local business

We are thrilled to announce that a local business has generously donated $10,000 to the Norwood Child Safety Councillors. This donation will go a long way in helping us to continue our work in promoting child safety in the community.

Thank you to the generous donor for their support, and we encourage others in the community to follow their lead in supporting our cause.

New safety video released for parents and children

We are excited to announce the release of our latest safety video for parents and children. The video covers a range of important topics, including stranger danger, road safety, and online safety.

We encourage all parents and caregivers to watch the video with their children, and to use it as a tool for discussing important safety messages. The video is available on our website and social media channels.

Volunteers needed for upcoming fundraiser event

We are seeking volunteers to assist with our upcoming fundraiser event. The event will be held on Saturday, April 15th, and will raise funds for our child safety programs and initiatives.

Volunteers will assist with a range of tasks, including event set-up and clean-up, ticket sales, and food service. If you are interested in volunteering, please contact us at info@ncsc.org.
