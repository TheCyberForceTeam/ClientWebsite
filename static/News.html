New safety video released for parents and children

Keeping children safe is a top priority for parents, but it can be challenging to teach children about safety without scaring them or overwhelming them with too much information. That's why a new safety video has been released for parents and children to help educate families about important safety topics in a fun and engaging way. The safety video is designed to be a resource for parents who want to teach their children about safety but aren't sure where to start. The video covers a range of safety topics, including fire safety, water safety, stranger danger, and internet safety. Each topic is presented in a way that is easy for children to understand and remember, and the video includes interactive elements to keep children engaged and interested. One of the benefits of this new safety video is that it's designed to be fun for children to watch. Instead of presenting safety information in a dry, boring way, the video features colorful animations and relatable characters that children can connect with. By presenting safety information in a fun and engaging way, children are more likely to remember the information and apply it in real-life situations.

We encourage all parents and caregivers to watch the video with their children, and to use it as a tool for discussing important safety messages. The video is available on our website and social media channels.
